Research Papers

Firmware in Flight: A Deep Dive into OTA Update Mechanisms for IoT

This research paper explores the design, security, and efficiency of Over-The-Air (OTA) firmware update systems in Internet of Things (IoT) devices. It examines how OTA mechanisms work, why they are essential for maintaining device functionality and security, and highlights the challenges such as limited bandwidth, power constraints, and security vulnerabilities. The paper also proposes improvements to existing OTA architectures to make updates more reliable, secure, and scalable for large IoT deployments.
DOI: https://doi.org/10.22214/ijraset.2025.69655
